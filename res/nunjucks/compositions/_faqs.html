  • Do I need to get a prescription from my doctor before calling Edgepark to place my order?

    Our online breast pump order form makes it easy for you to receive a breast pump through insurance. After you complete the order form, an Edgepark Customer Care Specialist will reach out to your doctor on your behalf and request a prescription. In the event your doctor does not respond in a timely manner, we may contact you for assistance.

    If you already have a paper copy of a breast pump prescription, it will help us process your order more quickly. You can scan and e-mail a copy of your prescription to pumpprescriptions@edgepark.com or fax it to 330-963-6839.
